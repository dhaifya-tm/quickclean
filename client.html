How it works

Drop off. Walk away. Get notified.

Four simple steps - no second trip, no awkward wait by the machines.

The transfer process
1
📲
Fill in your booking
Enter your name, phone, washer number and how many minutes are left on your cycle. Upload a photo of your laundry bag. Add a second washer if needed.
💡 Do this before or right after you start your wash.
2
🫧
Select your dryer and pay
Choose 1 or 2 dryer machines, set your duration (30-60 min), and pay via DuitNow QR. Upload your payment screenshot to confirm.
💡 Flat rate - RM3 for 1 machine, RM4 for 2 machines. No extra charges.
3
🔁
We handle the transfer
When your wash is done, our attendant moves your clothes to the dryer. You'll get a WhatsApp message the moment the transfer happens.
💡 Your clothes are only handled by an attendant matching your gender preference.
4
🔔
Collect when ready
When drying is done, you'll get an instant WhatsApp notification. One trip back, clean clothes waiting.
💡 Track everything live on your tracker page - washer, transfer, dryer and done.

Common questions
What if I forget to book before my cycle ends? ⌄
Book as early as possible - ideally before or right when you start the machine. For last-minute requests, message us on WhatsApp but it's not guaranteed during peak hours.
Can I choose a specific attendant? ⌄
You choose the gender of your attendant at booking. Specific person requests aren't available yet.
What if no dryer is free when my wash ends? ⌄
We'll notify you immediately and keep your clothes safe until a dryer slot opens - usually within 5-10 minutes.
Is this service only for Village 4? ⌄
Yes - Quick Clean currently operates in Village 4, UTP only.
How do I pay? ⌄
Pay via DuitNow QR directly in the app. Upload your payment screenshot to confirm your booking.
